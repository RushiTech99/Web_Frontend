OUR STORY

Once upon a time, in a cozy little bakery, Iyengar's Bakery was born out of a love for spreading smiles in the year 1981.

Our founder, Ramesh Iyengar, believed that the secret to happiness lies in the joy of sharing freshly baked goods with loved ones. Today, we continue to spread joy through our delightful range of artisanal breads, pastries, and cakes — all crafted with love.

HOUSE OF FRESHNESS

Iyengar's Bakery is your one-stop destination for freshly baked delights that combine tradition with innovation. Renowned for its wide range of cakes, bread, cookies, and snacks, we are committed to delivering high-quality products made with love and precision.
